  • Resource Name: Emergency Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
  • About: A national hotline used for those who need to connect with trained counselors for immediate emotional support during mental crises.
  • Access: Call or Text: 988


  • Resource Name: Massachusetts Behavioral Health Help Line
  • About: A free, confidential service that connects anyone in Massachusetts to immediate mental and behavioral health support, including help for anxiety, fear, or crisis related to school safety or violence.
  • Access: Call or Text: (833) 773-2445 or visit masshelpline.com


  • Resource Name: NFI Massachusetts Inc. (Haverhill)
  • About: A medical clinic in Haverhill that provides mental and behavioral health support for youth and families, offering counseling and comunity-based services.
  • Access: (978) 373-1181


  • Resource Name: Arbour Counseling Services (Haverhill)
  • About: A local counseling and behavioral-health clinic offering outpatient therapy, community-based support, and treatment programs for individuals, families, and couples to address anxiety, stress, and other mental health concerns.
  • Access: (978) 373-7010


  • Resource Name: Beth Israel Lahey Health Behavioral Services (Lawrence)
  • About: Provides outpatient mental health support and counseling for children, teens, and adults including help with anxiety, stress, and emotional safety concerns.
  • Access: (24/7 mobile crisis support) (978) 620-1250 / (877) 382-1609


  • Resource Name: Eliot North Shore Community Behavioral Health Center (Danvers)
  • About: A Community Behavioral Health Center offering 24/7 urgent and crisis support, individual and family therapy, stabilization services, and care coordination to help people of all ages manage anxiety, stress, trauma, and other mental health concerns.
  • Access: (888) 769-5201
