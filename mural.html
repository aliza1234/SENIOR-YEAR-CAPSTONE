Mural Proposal: Lotus of Growth

This mural is designed to symbolize resilience, growth, and remembrance. It visually connects the history of schools affected by gun violence to the ongoing journey of healing and awareness within our community.

Home About Schools Affected Resources Mural

Concept

The mural will feature a large lotus flower, symbolizing growth through difficulty. The lotus is an ideal symbol for students and community members navigating challenges, reminding us that beauty and strength emerge from struggle.

The stems of the lotus will be composed of the names of older schools affected by gun violence, honoring their memory and acknowledging the history. The petals will display word art of more recent schools affected, representing growth, awareness, and the ongoing impact.

The mural will also feature the powerful quote:

"I am no longer accepting the things I cannot change. I am changing the things I cannot accept." – Angela Davis

Purpose & Community Impact

This mural is intended to:

  • Honor the memory of students and staff affected by school gun violence.
  • Promote awareness of school safety and mental health resources.
  • Encourage students and the community to engage in positive change and dialogue.
  • Serve as a permanent symbol of growth, resilience, and hope for our school.
